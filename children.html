Alexis Karren was born 2016-08-05 at 18:36 MDT.  She weighed 5 pounds 1 ounce, and was 16.0 inches long.
Katherine Judith was born 2016-08-05 at 18:47 MDT.  She weighed 5 pounds 2 ounces, and was 16.1 inches long.

Katherine had a slightly low blood sugar level when she was born, but rebounded quickly and met all of her numbers by the next day.
Alexis met all of her numbers at first, but struggled to keep her blood sugar level up.  This meant she was fed a few milliliters of glucose from time to time.  It made her smell like a bakery, or cotton candy.  As if we needed a reason to love her more.

Mommy's temperature spiked to 38 degrees Celsius during labor (37.0 degrees Celsius is normal body temperature).  The doctor wanted antibiotics run on mother and child.  Alexis went to the nursery first.  Daddy followed her, while Katherine stayed with mommy and the four grandparents.  This meant dad got to pick out the bow to put in her hair.  Gold was chosen, because it was unique, and there was only one left.  When Katherine arrived in the nursery, dad chose silver for her.  Later it was realized that they were born on the opening day of the 2016 Olympics in Rio.  Alexis took gold, and Katherine took silver.

Cousin Ashton later told us that it was also the first day of National Twin Day 2016 (National Twin Day is the first full weekend in August, Friday to Sunday).

For their first checkup, both girls had gained weight!  Alexis had gained a half ounce, and Katherine had gained 2.5 ounces.  They also each gained more than an inch in length.  I suspect that the delivery room length measurements may have been less than accurate.
Alexis had slightly elevated bilirubin levels, and had to be checked daily via a blood draw from her heel.  She slept through all four draws.
Katherine had a slight heart murmur, and had to have an echo cardiogram.  That revealed a small problem with her pulmonary valve.  This is nothing major, but needs to be rechecked in three months.
